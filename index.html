Animal Collector Game

Instructions

You will be given a Target Score chosen as a random number at the start of the game.

There are four animals below.
By clicking on an animal you will add a specific amount of points to your Total Score.

You win the game by matching Your Total Score to the Target Score.
You lost the game if Your Total Score goes above the Target Score.

The value of each animal is hidden from you until you click on it.
